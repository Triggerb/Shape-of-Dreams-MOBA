[h1]Master Wu[/h1]
An early playable build of an original fast melee Traveler.

Version 0.3 temporarily converts Shell into Master Wu while the project researches
the prefab, resource-registry, save-data, and networking work required for a
separate ninth Traveler. Select Shell in the lobby to play Master Wu.

This greybox adds a fourth-hit Double Strike cadence, Wuju-style pure on-hit
damage, a physical AD-scaling chained Vanishing Cut, and a movement/attack-speed
Unbound Tempo stance.
